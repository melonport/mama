Multichain Asset Managers Association

MAMA is a trade body, initiated by Melonport AG, which represents asset management companies, investors, technology providers, service providers and ecosystem players interested in working towards a new vision for asset management using blockchain and other supporting decentralised technologies.

What we believed

Our Vision

We believe MAM technology has the real potential to hasten a [sweeping] transformation of the Asset Management industry, democratizing and streamlining the cumbersome processes and infrastructure upon which the global financial system runs today.

The innovators emerging around MAM technology have a truly unique window of opportunity to redesign a new frontier of financial infrastructure by incorporating the latest technology without the burden of backward compatibility and legacy systems.

Governance systems and regulation designed around these new technologies will play a significant role in the mechanisms of future decision-making and dispute resolution. Already, new examples of governance systems are beginning to surface that can, through pioneering technical design and voting processes, empower even the most vulnerable participants in an ecosystem.

It is also becoming clear that the adoption MAM technology will save investors and asset managers significant resources in terms of effort, time and capital, leading to increased transparency, seamless regulatory reporting and a drastic reduction in the scope for human error.

Protocol tokens such as Bitcoin or Ethereum are to a large degree already recognized as an asset class in their own right, and we believe they are here to stay. However, we fundamentally have a much broader vision for MAMA. We believe that every individual traditional asset class (e.g. equities, commodities, bonds, real-estate, etc.) has the potential to be “digitized” into the “token class”, and thus, the universe of tradable digital assets within MAM will continue to expand exponentially as these assets seek to capture blockchain efficiencies and benefits.

This evolution will require a similar evolution of fit-for-purpose MAM software. Our vision is that it will soon be possible to hold more assets in a MAM-regulated and administered fund, than it is in a traditional investment account today.

The transformation is happening. It is happening fast and it is the intention of MAMA to support the industry through the change as stipulated in the Goals below.
